  • ETV Big Filmnight

    Estonian Public Broadcasting ‐ Director / Researcher

    Apr 2007

    Estonian Public Broadcasting’s “ETV´s Big Film Night” is 5 hour long programme which shows selected film students work made in previous year in Estonia. The programme includes comments of the young directors and is considered to be one of the stepping stones for young Estonian film directors. Responsibilities additional to creative input included scripting and coordinating the work of creative team and researching the subjects to interview. Overseeing the flow with editorial materials on the project.
